Ducti attend to the operational, day-to-day activities of the pack and serve as warleaders. They are responsible for the combat training and functionality of the pack, as well as its safety and security. In most packs, the Ductus is the primary authority. However, in packs with a more religious bent, the Priest may hold primary authority, so long as the pack is not actively preparing for, or engaging in, combat. A Ductus may administer physical punishment to any member of her pack. For more information on sect punishments, see the list on page 419. A Ductus gains the abiding status trait Blessed during her tenure. A Ductus holds status jurisdiction over the members of her pack.
