Why would any Sabbat support this?
Bad faith. They're hoping to get these Tremere into a vulnerable position and kill them all.
Information about Tremere. The Telyav were experts in Tremere magics from the Dark Ages. They have no idea exactly how the Tremere work today. Jaccar and his cohort could give the Sabbat the information required to take down the Inner Council, a group of Antediluvians they didn't even know about as a target until now.
Emancipation. The Vaulderie can bring everybody closer to Caine. It's meant to purify and free Cainites from bondage. Very few Cainites are more enslaved than the Tremere. This is, from a Loyalist perspective, the point of the Sabbat. What are we if we fail in that obligation now?
Magical toys. It's harder to remember this mechanics are so narrow in BNS, but these Tremere presumably have access and the ability to create magical items it a scale that the Telyav can't.
Information about the Camarilla. These guys have active, current information about what's going on with the Camarilla and its vulnerabilities.
Allies on Humanity. The Order of St Blaise, in general, would love an influx of True Sabbat who have no attachments to Paths of Enlightenment. If they have a bunch of Technomancers, even better. The Order becomes a natural place for them and the Vicar would advocate for this strongly.
War is Peace. For those that still care about any cease-fires (which is the big part of the plot that everybody basically ignores in Sabbat games), this is a way to cut at the Camarilla's numbers without breaking that treaty. Tremere are extremely valuable assets to the Camarilla so being able to essentially neutralize a chunk of them with a few concessions and new recruits. Why not?
